Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Private Clinic App is a private patient management tool for acupuncture and Chinese medicine practitioners. This policy describes how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

We collect your Google account email address and name for authentication purposes. Patient data entered into the app is stored securely in our database and is accessible only to authorized practitioners.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the app's functionality: managing patient records, scheduling, and session notes. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

Google Calendar Access

If you grant calendar access, we use it only to sync appointments. We do not read or modify unrelated calendar events.

Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in a secure, encrypted database hosted by Supabase.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the app administrator.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact the app administrator.
